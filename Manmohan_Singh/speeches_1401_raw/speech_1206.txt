August 19, 2012New Delhi



PM's address at the Rajiv Gandhi National Sadbhavana Award Ceremony
The Prime Minister Dr. Manmohan Singh addressed the ceremony to present Rajiv Gandhi Sadbhavana Award 2012 ceremony here today. The Award was given to Shri D. R. Mehta in recognition of his work through the Bhagwan Mahaveer Viklang Sahayata Samiti, Jaipur.
