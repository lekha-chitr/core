PM's address at the Rajiv Gandhi National Sadbhavana Award Ceremony
The Prime Minister Dr. Manmohan Singh addressed the ceremony to present Rajiv Gandhi Sadbhavana Award ceremony here today. The Award given to SPIC MACAY ('Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music and Culture amongst Youth').
